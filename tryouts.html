Tryout With The Club

Open to all men attending Texas Tech University!

The goal of the TTU Men's Volleyball Club is to grow the game of volleyball.

The team is always open to new members looking to sharpen their skills, compete at a high level, or learn the basics!

We maintain a competitive mindset with the intention of helping every member improve and work as a team.

Requirements

Players must have the following requirements to participate in club tryouts:

Athletic Shoes (recommend basketball shoes or ASICS)

Signed Waiver Form

$5 Tryout Fee

Recommended items for tryouts include:

Bring Water (limited fountains near court)

Remove Jewelry or Watches

Stretch before & after playing

Location

Located on the main floor of the University Rec Center, "Courts 7 & 8". Head towards the main gym, through the basketball courts, to the last volleyball net against the wall.

*Image taken above designated court*

General Information

Club activities are held year round, but participants must tryout on the specified dates and times before being placed on a team or joining practices.

$5 Fee REQUIRED to tryout.

The Texas Tech Men's Volleyball Club requires a "$300" deposit to cover team costs and uniforms. Said deposit is only required once a player is designated to a roster position, and can be refunded if the player decides not to play for the Club before the first tournament.
