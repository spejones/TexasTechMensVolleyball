General Information

We encourage players to make informed decisions about the commitment of the club before paying the deposit.
If you have questions please feel free to ask us.

Club activities are held year round, but participants must tryout on the specified dates and times before being placed on a team or joining practices.

$5 Fee REQUIRED to Tryout.

The Texas Tech Men's Volleyball Club requires a "$300" deposit to cover team cost and uniforms. Said deposit is only required once a player is designated to a roster position, and can be refunded if the player decides not to play for the Club before the first tournament.
