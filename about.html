About Us

Our Story

The Texas Tech Men's Volleyball Club is a student organization that operaters through the University Recreation Sport Clubs Program. Our purpose is to motivate and teach the game of volleyball. While doing so, we keep a competitive mindset with the intentions of improving every member and working as a team.


The goal of the TTU Men's Volleyball Club is to grow the game of volleyball. The team is always open to new members wanting to sharpen their skills, play a competitive sport, or learn the basics!
