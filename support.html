Our Story

All donations go towards travel expenses, gear, and tournament fees. Those who would like to support the team can donate through the Texas Tech Men's Volleyball GoFundMe.

Thanks to all your donations, last year was the first time the TTU Men's Volleyball Club was able to send both an A and B team to Nationals in Arizona!

With your help this upcoming season, the club aims to bring both teams back to nationals for another shot at the title!
